The overall ecosystem is comprised of 3 layers: the protocol, the service layer, and the application layer.
The Protocol is entirely composed of Ethereum smart contracts that allow for the creation and validation of events, the issuance and sale of tickets in primary and secondary ticket markets (customisable by the event organisers), and the distribution of ticket sale revenue and market/event fees between the event organisers, ticket promoters, market matchers, attendees, etc. There will be minimal fees for using these contracts so that the protocol can remain competitive and enjoy maximal adoption.
Is intended to be a free and open project, the protocol specification being under a Creative Commons license and the code being placed under a FLOSS license.
